Typhoid Information

About Typhoid

Typhoid fever is a bacterial infection caused by Salmonella typhi. It is spread through contaminated food, water, or close contact with an infected person.

Causes of Typhoid

Typhoid fever is caused by the Salmonella typhi bacterium. It spreads through the ingestion of contaminated food or water contaminated with feces from an infected person.

Symptoms of Typhoid

  • Prolonged fever
  • Weakness and fatigue
  • Abdominal pain
  • Headache
  • Poor appetite
  • Rash
  • Constipation or diarrhea

Home Remedies for Typhoid

1. Drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated.

2. Get plenty of rest to help your body recover.

3. Eat small, light meals to ease digestive discomfort.

4. Use lukewarm water for bathing to reduce fever.

5. Drink herbal teas like ginger or chamomile for symptom relief.

Medicines for Typhoid

Common medicines for treating typhoid fever include:

  • Antibiotics such as ciprofloxacin or azithromycin
  • Antipyretics like acetaminophen to reduce fever
  • Anti-diarrheal medications for diarrhea relief

Diet Recommendations for Typhoid

During typhoid fever, it's important to eat foods that are easy to digest and provide essential nutrients. Some recommended foods include:

  • Cooked vegetables
  • Boiled rice or porridge
  • Cottage cheese
  • Bananas
  • Clear soups and broths

Avoid spicy, greasy, or heavy foods that may irritate the stomach.
