Dengue Information

About Dengue

Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection that causes flu-like symptoms, including fever, headache, muscle and joint pains, and a characteristic skin rash. In severe cases, it can lead to dengue hemorrhagic fever, which can be fatal.

Causes of Dengue

Dengue is caused by the dengue virus, which is transmitted to humans through the bite of infected Aedes mosquitoes, primarily Aedes aegypti.

Symptoms of Dengue

  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Muscle and joint pains
  • Severe abdominal pain
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Weakness and fatigue
  • Skin rash
  • Bleeding from the nose or gums

Home Remedies for Dengue

1. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of fluids.

2. Get plenty of rest to help your body recover.

3. Use pain relievers like acetaminophen for fever and pain (avoid aspirin and ibuprofen).

4. Apply cold compresses to reduce fever and alleviate discomfort.

5. Eat nutritious foods to support your immune system.

Medicines for Dengue

There are no specific antiviral medications for dengue. Treatment typically focuses on relieving symptoms and supporting the immune system.

Consult a healthcare professional for appropriate management of symptoms and complications.

Diet Recommendations for Dengue

During dengue fever, it's important to eat foods that are easy to digest and provide essential nutrients. Some recommended foods include:

  • Fruits like papaya, orange, and kiwi
  • Vegetables like spinach, broccoli, and carrots
  • Whole grains like brown rice and quinoa
  • Lean proteins such as chicken, fish, and tofu
  • Fluids like coconut water, herbal teas, and broth

Avoid spicy, oily, and processed foods, as they can exacerbate digestive issues.
