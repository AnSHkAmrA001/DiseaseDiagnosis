Pneumonia Information

About Pneumonia

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. The air sacs may fill with fluid or pus, causing cough with phlegm or pus, fever, chills, and difficulty breathing.

Causes of Pneumonia

Pneumonia can be caused by bacteria, viruses, or fungi. Common causes include Streptococcus pneumoniae bacteria, influenza viruses, and respiratory syncytial virus (RSV).

Symptoms of Pneumonia

  • Cough with phlegm or pus
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Chest pain when breathing or coughing
  • Fast heartbeat
  • Fatigue
  • Nausea or vomiting

Home Remedies for Pneumonia

1. Get plenty of rest.

2. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of fluids.

3. Use a humidifier to moisten the air.

4. Take over-the-counter pain relievers for fever and discomfort.

5. Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke.

Medicines for Pneumonia

Common medicines for treating pneumonia include:

  • Antibiotics to treat bacterial pneumonia
  • Antiviral medications for viral pneumonia
  • Antifungal drugs for fungal pneumonia
  • Cough medicine to relieve cough symptoms
  • Fever reducers such as acetaminophen or ibuprofen

Diet Recommendations for Pneumonia

During pneumonia, it's important to eat nutritious foods to support your recovery. Some recommended foods include:

  • Chicken soup or broth
  • Whole grains like oatmeal or brown rice
  • Lean proteins such as chicken, turkey, or fish
  • Fruits and vegetables high in vitamins and antioxidants
  • Yogurt or kefir for probiotics

Avoid sugary and processed foods, as they can weaken the immune system.
